Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Wirdly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Wirdly (the "App").

Key Point: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Reminders: Your wird reminders, including titles, descriptions, schedules, images, and completion history
  • Settings: Your app preferences, theme selections, and notification settings
  • Progress Data: Streak counts, completion dates, and total completions for each reminder
  • Tasbeeh Counts: Your tasbeeh counter data (if used)

Location Data

We use your device's location to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your area
  • Provide location-based prayer time notifications

Important: Your location coordinates are sent to the Aladhan API (a third-party prayer time service) to retrieve prayer times. We do not store your location data on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide prayer time calculations based on your location
  • To send you reminder notifications at scheduled times
  • To display your reminders and track your progress
  • To personalize your app experience (themes, settings)

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Transmit your location or reminders to external servers (except for prayer time API calls)
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Third-Party Services

Aladhan API

We use the Aladhan API for prayer time calculations:

  • Your location (coordinates only) is sent to this API to retrieve prayer times
  • Please review Aladhan's privacy policy for their data practices
  • We do not control how Aladhan handles this data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All data is stored locally on your device and accessible through the app
  • Delete your data: Delete reminders or clear app data at any time through your device settings
  • Opt-out of location services: Use manual location entry instead of automatic location detection
  • Disable notifications: Turn off notifications in app settings or device settings

Permissions

Location Permission

  • Required for: Automatic prayer time calculation
  • Optional: You can deny this permission and use manual location entry instead
  • Usage: Location is only accessed when calculating prayer times

Notification Permission

  • Required for: Prayer time and reminder notifications
  • Control: You can disable notifications in app settings or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings

Photo Library Permission

  • Required for: Adding images to reminders (optional feature)
  • Control: You can deny this permission and use URL-based images instead

Camera Permission

  • Required for: Taking photos for reminders (optional feature)
  • Control: You can deny this permission and use other image sources instead

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using standard device encryption
  • No data is transmitted over insecure connections
  • We do not have access to your stored data
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Notifying you through the app if significant changes are made

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Consent
