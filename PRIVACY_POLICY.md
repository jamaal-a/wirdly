# Wirdly Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## Introduction

Wirdly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Wirdly (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- We collect your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area
- Location data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- You can choose to use manual location entry instead of GPS

### Reminder Data
- We store your wird reminders, spiritual practice data, and preferences locally on your device
- This data includes reminder titles, descriptions, schedules, images, and completion tracking
- All reminder data is stored locally using device storage (AsyncStorage) and is not transmitted to external servers

### Settings Data
- We store your app preferences (calculation methods, notification settings, etc.) locally on your device
- Settings are saved using device storage and are not shared externally

### Prayer Times
- We fetch prayer times from third-party APIs (Aladhan API) based on your location
- Prayer times are cached locally for offline access
- We do not store or transmit your prayer time queries

## How We Use Your Information

- **Prayer Time Calculation**: Location data is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times
- **Reminder Functionality**: Reminder data is used to send you notifications at scheduled times
- **App Functionality**: Settings are used to customize your app experience
- **Offline Access**: Cached prayer times allow the app to work without an internet connection

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device
- We use AsyncStorage for local data persistence
- Data is never transmitted to our servers
- Data remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear app data

## Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Transmit your location or reminders to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services

## Third-Party Services

### Aladhan API
- We use the Aladhan API for prayer time calculations
- Your location (coordinates only) is sent to this API to retrieve prayer times
- Please review [Aladhan's privacy policy](https://aladhan.com/privacy) for their data practices

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by deleting reminders or clearing app data)
- Opt-out of location services (use manual location entry)
- Disable notifications (in app settings)

## Permissions

### Location Permission
- Required for automatic prayer time calculation
- You can deny this permission and use manual location entry instead
- Location is only accessed when calculating prayer times

### Notification Permission
- Required for prayer time and reminder notifications
- You can disable notifications in app settings
- You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings

### Photo Library Permission
- Required only if you want to add images to reminders
- You can deny this permission and use URL-based images instead

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- We use standard device encryption for stored data
- No data is transmitted over insecure connections

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
