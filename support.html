‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a wird reminder?

Go to the Wirds screen and tap the "Add New Reminder" button. Fill in the details like title, description, schedule type (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or prayer time), and set your preferred time.

How do I sync reminders with prayer times?

When creating a reminder, select "Prayer Time" as the schedule type, then choose which salah (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, or Isha) you want to sync with. The reminder will automatically trigger at that prayer time.

Notifications are not working. What should I do?

  • Check that notifications are enabled in your device Settings ‚Üí Wirdly ‚Üí Notifications
  • Make sure the app has notification permissions
  • Ensure your reminder is set to "Active" in the app
  • Check that your device is not in Do Not Disturb mode
  • Restart the app if needed

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Appearance ‚Üí Theme. You can choose from Original, Dark Mode, or Calm Mode.

How do I use the Tasbeeh counter?

Go to the Tasbeeh tab and tap the counter area to increment. Set a target count if desired, and tap the reset button to start over.

Can I add images to my reminders?

Yes! When creating or editing a reminder, you can add an image by taking a photo, selecting from your photo library, or entering an image URL.

How do I track my progress?

Each reminder shows your streak count and total completions. Mark reminders as complete when you finish them, and your progress will be tracked automatically.

My prayer times seem incorrect. How can I fix this?

Prayer times are calculated based on your device location. Make sure location services are enabled for Wirdly. You can also manually set your location in the app settings if needed.

How do I delete a reminder?

Go to the Wirds screen, find the reminder you want to delete, and tap on it. Then tap the delete/trash icon to remove it.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes! All your data (reminders, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit your personal information to external servers.
